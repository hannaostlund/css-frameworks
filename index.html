Premium Trees, Direct to You

Scandi Tree is devoted to delivering the essence of Scandinavian tradition to your doorstep. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we specialize in providing hand-selected, eco-friendly Christmas trees, ensuring cherished moments and authentic holiday magic for your home.

Nordmann Fir

Indulge in the beauty of the Nordmann Fir, known for its lush foliage and needle retention. Revel in its luxurious appearance, bringing elegance and lasting freshness to your festive celebrations.

Norway Spruce

Evoke nostalgia with the classic charm of the Norway Spruce. Adorn your home with its rich, traditional scent and iconic conical shape, capturing the essence of a timeless Christmas.

Mini Tree

Embrace the delightful charm of our Mini Tree, a perfect addition to any space. Experience the joy of the season with this adorable and compact Christmas companion, spreading festive cheer wherever it stands.
